Welcome To Gami Palm Amore

Gami Palm Amore is an excellently designed gated community along the Palm Beach Road in Seawoods. The multiple elevations comprise a range of 2 BHK, 3 BHK, 4 BHK, and 5 BHK apartments. These spacious apartments come with an extensive deck in the living room and a balcony in the master bedroom. The designer residences exhibit a blend of exclusivity and grace. This gated community has a 24/7 water supply and power backup for common areas and lifts. Moreover, it also features 43+ exquisite lifestyle amenities for recreation, social wellness, fitness, and entertainment. It includes a swimming pool with deck, kids’ play area, squash court, badminton court, gymnasium with a dedicated gym for women, yoga lawn, banquet hall, reading room, music library, etc.

Situated in the esteemed neighbourhood of the Palm Beach Road, it has some of the finest social landmarks. DAV Public School, Delhi Public School, Jewel of Navi Mumbai, DY Patil University, Apollo Hospital, Podar International School, Healing Hands Clinic, Seawoods Grand Central Mall, Dmart, Zudio, and Wonder’s Park are nearby. Being adjacent to the Palm Beach Road, it is well-connected to the commercial hubs of Navi Mumbai. Sion Panvel Highway and Seawoods Railway Station are 5-10 minutes away. The Atal Setu and the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport are just a 20-minute drive away via the Airport Road. So, embrace exclusivity with the designer residences of Gami Palm Amore in Seawoods.

About Gami Group

Gami Group is one of the renowned developers in Navi Mumbai for its distinctive architecture. The group is known for its customer-centric services and state-of-the-art residential and commercial spaces. With 23+ years of excellence, it has delivered 25+ projects with 5.9 M+ sq. ft. under construction.Born with a philosophy to create lifestyles that are imagined, desired and wished for; Gami Group was established in the year 2000.
